PRODUCT CATEGORY: HOME THERMOSTATS

Product ID: HW-TH-T4PRO
Name: Honeywell Home T4 Pro Programmable Thermostat (TH4110U2005)
Description: 7-day programmable thermostat with four programmable time periods per day and separate weekday/weekend schedules. Features adaptive intelligent recovery that learns how long the HVAC system takes to reach a target temperature and turns on heating or cooling early so the home is comfortable at the scheduled time. Includes compressor protection that forces a multi-minute delay before restart to prevent damage.
Power: Two AA alkaline batteries (recommended); low-battery icon appears approximately two months before replacement is required. When battery power is critically low, only the BATT indicator, alert icon, and battery icon remain visible.
System Compatibility: 24 volt heating and cooling systems only. NOT compatible with 120/240 volt line voltage systems.
Temperature Range:
- Heat: 40 F to 90 F (4.5 C to 32 C)
- Cool: 50 F to 99 F (10 C to 37 C)
System Modes: Heat, Cool, Off, and Emergency Heat (TH4210U only)
Fan Modes: Auto (fan runs only when heating or cooling is active) and On (fan runs continuously)
Keypad Lockout: OFF (unlocked), PART (temperature changes allowed only), ON (no access). Unlock password is 1234.
Package Includes: T4 Pro thermostat, UWP mounting system, decorative cover plate, screws and anchors, two AA batteries, literature.

Product ID: HW-TH-RTH6500WF
Name: Honeywell Wi-Fi 7-Day Programmable Thermostat (RTH6500WF / RTH65801006)
Description: Wi-Fi connected programmable thermostat with remote access through the Honeywell Home app via a Comfort account. Pre-set with energy-saving programs for four time periods; default settings can reduce heating and cooling expenses by as much as 33% when used as directed (savings vary by region and usage).
Power Requirement: Requires a C (common) wire to supply 24 VAC power to the thermostat. Many older mechanical or battery-only thermostats do not have a C wire; check wifithermostat.com for adapter guidance.
System Modes: Heat, Cool, Off, EM HEAT (for heat pumps with auxiliary heat; compressor is locked out in this mode)
Setup Process: Install thermostat, connect to home Wi-Fi network, register online for remote access. During setup the thermostat broadcasts an SSID in the form "NewThermostat_123456" which the user connects to from a phone or computer.
Safety Notice: Contains a lithium battery that may contain perchlorate material. Special handling may apply.
Voltage Compatibility: 24 VAC systems only; will NOT work on 120/240 volt systems.

PRODUCT CATEGORY: COMMERCIAL BUILDING CONTROLS

Product ID: HW-CB-TB3026B
Name: Honeywell BACnet Fixed Function Thermostat (TB3026B)
Description: Configurable commercial thermostat with 19 pre-loaded applications for fan coil, heat pump, and conventional rooftop HVAC systems. Communicating intelligent sensor-controller with built-in temperature and humidity sensors. Operates as a fully functioning BACnet controller over an MS/TP LAN for integration with building automation systems. A wireless sensor variant (TB3026B-W) is also available.
Communications: BACnet MS/TP over EIA-485 twisted-pair. Each unit exerts 1/4 unit load on the MS/TP LAN.
Transmission Speeds: 9.6, 19.2, 38.4, or 76.8 Kbps (configured at the global controller)
Network Topology: Bus (daisy chain)
Maximum Segment Length: 4000 ft (1071 m) using recommended cable; repeaters required beyond this distance, with a maximum of three repeaters between any two devices.
Maximum Devices: 128 master devices maximum per network; 255 total devices (master plus slave) overall.
Cabling Requirements: Shielded twisted-pair with characteristic impedance between 100 and 130 ohms. Distributed capacitance between conductors must be less than 30 pF/foot (100 pF/m).
Terminating Resistors: Matched precision resistors, 1/4 W, +/-1%, 80-130 ohms, wired across MS/TP+ and MS/TP- at each end of the segment.
Installation Height: Mount approximately 4 ft (1.2 m) above the floor in an area with good air circulation at average temperature. Confirm mounting height meets Americans with Disabilities Act requirements.
Wire Gauge: Maximum and recommended 18 AWG; do not use wire smaller than 22 AWG.
Power: 24 VAC on terminal 1; a factory-installed jumper between pins 1 and 2 supplies 24 VAC to relays BO-1, BO-3, and BO-4.
Installation Warning: Installer must be a trained, experienced service technician. Disconnect power supply before installation to avoid electrical shock or equipment damage.

PRODUCT CATEGORY: INDUSTRIAL GAS DETECTION

Product ID: HW-GD-XNX
Name: Honeywell XNX Universal Transmitter
Description: A high-specification universal gas transmitter compatible with a wide range of Honeywell Analytics local or remote gas detectors for flammable, toxic, and oxygen gas hazards. Uses a common platform with modular input and output options. Offers more than 200 unique configurations and is certified for hazardous areas including Zone 1 and 2, Zone 21 and 22, and North American Class I and II Division 1 or 2.
Supported Sensor Technologies: Electrochemical (EC), Infrared (IR), and Catalytic Bead (MPD)
Combustible Gas Detection Range: 0 to 100% LEL/LFL
Housing Material: Painted LM25 aluminum standard; 316 stainless steel with marine-grade coating optional
Operating Temperature: -40 C to +65 C (-40 F to +150 F), depending on sensor type. Accuracy between -20 C and -40 C is +/- 30% of applied gas. Continuous operation at temperatures below -20 C for more than 12 hours may cause sensor deterioration.
Operating Humidity: 20 to 90% relative humidity, non-condensing
Dimensions: 160 mm x 197 mm x 114 mm (compact); L370 mm x W280 mm x D180 mm with flow housing
Weight: 2.8 kg (LM25 aluminum alloy)
Power: 24 VDC nominal
Output Signal: 4-20 mA with HART 6.0 protocol (standard)
Optional Output Modules (mutually exclusive with each other but compatible with 4-20mA/HART):
- Relay Module: Three user-configurable relays (2 x SPCO alarm relays, 1 x SPCO fault relay), SPDT, factory-installed in display module
- Modbus Module: Isolated RS-485 output for connection to multi-drop Modbus RTU networks
- Foundation Fieldbus Module: Digital communications interface for multi-drop Foundation Fieldbus H1 network
Cable Entries: Five threaded ports, available as 5 x M25 (ATEX/IECEx) or 5 x 3/4" NPT (UL/CSA)
Display: 2.5 inch high-resolution custom backlit LCD dot matrix showing gas reading, gas name and units, fault and alarm status, large numeric concentration or LEL display, and bar graph
User Interface: Non-intrusive four-button magnetic interface (operated with an external magnetic wand, no enclosure opening required)
Remote Sensor Option: Up to 50 ft (15.2 m) from the transmitter for catalytic bead and toxic sensors
Industry Applications: Upstream and downstream oil and gas, chemical, wastewater, and other industrial settings
Standard Supply: XNX transmitter with integral wall mounting lugs, five cable entries, magnetic wand/screwdriver, Allen key, three blanking plugs, quick start guide, and manual CD. MPD or XNX EC sensors are fitted to the bottom entry if ordered with the unit; other sensors are supplied separately.
Ordering: To order XNX parts, call Honeywell at 800-538-0363.
